Changelog:

  • 18-08-2019 (patch 2):
    • Added double draw card. Please read the card effect under the help page.
    • Fixed bug where the sidebar's settings were not saved.
    • Fixed bug where steal turn could not be played if the previous card was also steal turn.
    • Reduced the number of magnetic polarities in deck from 16 to 8.
  • 18-08-2019 (patch 1):
    • Added extra set of normal cards on top of the current deck (fix ration wild:normal).
    • Added navbar.
    • Added user settings page (users can mute buzz notifications, turn on notify when my turn to play...)
    • Sidebar settings and user settings are now saved per browser (will stay the same after page refresh)
    • Bug fix.
    • Max number of cards to ban increased to 5.
  • 17-08-2019:
    • Added new help page
    • Nerfed charity. In order to play this card you must draw a card.
    • Nerfed discard color. In order to play this card you must draw a card.
    • Nerfed discard wildcards. In order to play this card you must draw a card.
    • Nerfed discard numbers. In order to play this card you must draw a card. Now can discard 2 numbers instead of 3.
    • Duel card always results in drawing 3 cards.
    • Russian Roulette now results in drawing 3 cards, not 6.
    • Blackjack changed. If the player hits blackjack he will discard 4 cards (previously 5). If the player hits number higher than 21 he will draw 6 cards (previously 7)
  • 16-08-2019:
    • Fixed bug where you couldn't play a wildcard, but instead you had to draw a card.

  • 13-08-2019:
