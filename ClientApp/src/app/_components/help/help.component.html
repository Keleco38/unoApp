BlackHole = all cards in all hands are shuffled into a pile and then dealt to each person with the same number they had before.
DiscardWildCards =discard all wildcards from all player's hands from the game
StealTurn =can be played anytime; steal someone's turn and rotation continues from the user.
SwapHands = swap hands with the targeted user
DoubleEdge = target a person. He/she draws 5 cards while the user draws 2 cards
DiscardColor = user names a color. The chosen color should be discarded from all player's current hand.
HandOfGod = an only be used when cards on hand are more than 8. (counted after use) Immediately discard 4 cards from hand. Acts as normal wildcard otherwise.
Judgement = target a person. If they have a wildcard on their hand, they draw 3 cards, if not, nothing.
UnitedWeFall = Everyone draws 2 cards
ParadigmShift = each person swaps hands to the person next to them according to the current direction of the game.
Deflect = automatically reflects draw effects of wildcards back to the person that played it. This card is played automatically if you are targeted by the draw wildcard. Discards upon use. If used on draw +4 , draw +2, judgement or double edge, the person that played the attacking card receives the effect. Acts as normal wildcard otherwise.
InspectHand = select a person. That person must show their hand to you
GraveDigger = Take one card from the discarded pile.
RussianRoulette = Based on the direction of the game, players roll a dice. The first player that rolls 1 must draw 6 cards. Cannot be deflected.
Roulette = Randomly chosen player will either draw or discard X number of cards. Maximum number of cards to draw is 4, while the maximum number of cards to discard is 2. Cannot be deflected.
Duel = Player picks 3 numbers (from 1-6) and picks another player to duel. Dice is rolled afterwards. If the player guessed correctly the number that was rolled, he is a winner and the opponent must draw the biggest number he selected (from 3 numbers), otherwise he lost and he must draw the biggest number he selected. Cannot be deflected.
KeepMyHand = this card is automatically played from your hand in case of "black hole", "swap hands" or "paradigm shift". Your cards remains intact.
FairPlay = Target another player. If they have more cards than you, they discard until they have the same number as you. If they have less cards than you, they draw until the same number as you.
TheLastStand = if someone places their last card when a person has this card in their hand, this card will be automatically played and that person's win will be negated by drawing 2 new cards.. Acts as normal wildcard otherwise
Charity = Target a player and select two cards from your hand. The targeted player will receive this two cards as a gift. Cannot be deflected. Cannot be played if the number of the cards in your hand is less than 3 (2 cards + this charity card).
TricksOfTheTrade = Target a player. Exchange the random cards (random number, range: 1-3) from your hand with the targeted player.
